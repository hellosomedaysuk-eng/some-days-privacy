Privacy Policy

Some Days  ·  Last updated: July 2026

Some Days does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Everything you enter in the app — your name, your habits, your daily check-ins — stays entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. There is no account, no sign-up, and no way for us to see your data, because we never receive it.

What the app stores

Your habits, floors, daily energy check-ins, and optional first name. These are saved locally using your phone's built-in storage and are never shared.

Third-party services

The app uses no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising networks, and no tracking of any kind.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.
